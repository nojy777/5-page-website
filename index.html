About Me

I live in Texas. I have a daughter. She is one of my greatest reasons for wanting to be the best of me always. Before you learn more about me, I thought you should know that having a growth mindset is the key to success and that is what the picture on my page is about. I am excited to introduce myself. I hold 2 degrees, one in Accounting and Finance and the other one in Business. I have worked in the Healthcare, Finance, and Education sectors. I have worked as an Adult Education ESL Instructor. I also have experience working with and manipulating data using tools like Tableau and PowerBi. I am also knowledgeable with some programming languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. I have also worked as a Risk and Compliance Analyst. My certifications include Special Education, American Medical Billing, and Coding Certification, (AMBA), International Software Testing Qualification Board, (ISTQB), and Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL4) certifications. I love learning new things and meeting people. Reading and research are also what I enjoy. It comes naturally to me. I enjoy cooking and creating my own recipes. I love spending time with my family.
